A little about me

Aagaye aap?

I'm currently muddying my boots across CA Final curriculum pathways while finishing up my articleship training routines at BGJC & Associates LLP.

Like most students, I started with textbooks, lectures, and the hope that one day everything would somehow connect. Then articleship happened. Warehouses replaced case studies. Processes replaced assumptions. And suddenly every business had its own unique way of keeping scores.

Over time, I've had the opportunity to work across different industries, understand how businesses operate beyond reports and presentations, and learn that small process gaps often tell bigger stories than large presentations. Most days I'm still learning, still asking questions, and still discovering how much there is left to understand.
